# SHIV Mobile Sales Dashboard

Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes mobile sales performance for SHIV Mobile Sales across multiple dimensions such as time (Year, Quarter, Month, Day), city, brand, mobile model, payment method, and customer ratings. The dashboard is designed to help business users quickly understand sales trends, customer behavior, and overall performance using interactive visuals and filters.

Dashboard Features

 1. Sales Performance Analysis

* Total sales tracked by **Year, Quarter, Month, and Day**
* **MTD (Month-to-Date)** sales trend for detailed daily performance
* Comparison with **Same Period Last Year (YoY analysis)**

2. Geographic Insights

* **Total Sales by City** using map and pie charts
* Major contribution from cities like **Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore**

3. Product Analysis

* **Total Sales by Brand** (Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, Xiaomi)
* **Total Sales by Mobile Model** highlighting top-performing models

4. Payment Method Analysis

* Transactions split across **UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, and Cash**
* Balanced contribution across digital and cash payments

5. Customer Rating Analysis

* Sales distribution by **Rating Status (Good, Average, Poor)**
* Majority of sales come from **Good-rated transactions**, indicating high customer satisfaction

6. Time-Based Patterns

* **Sales by Day Name** (Weekday vs Weekend trends)
* Monthly and quarterly seasonality analysis

 
 Key Insights

* **Delhi and Mumbai** are the top revenue-generating cities
* **Apple and Samsung** lead in total sales value
* **Good-rated products** contribute over **60% of total sales**, reflecting strong product quality
* **UPI and Card payments** dominate transactions, showing strong digital adoption
* Sales show a **consistent upward MTD trend**, indicating healthy business growth
* Year-over-Year comparison shows **improvement in overall sales performance**

---

## ✅ Conclusion

The SHIV Mobile Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of sales performance across products, regions, and time periods. It enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor real-time and historical sales trends
* Identify top-performing cities, brands, and models
* Understand customer satisfaction through rating analysis
* Compare current performance with previous years for better planning

This dashboard can be effectively used by **sales managers, business analysts, and decision-makers** to drive data-backed strategies, optimize inventory, and improve customer experience.
